## 💰 PAYMENT FLOW:
1. User clicks "Unlock Premium"
2. Sees your plans (₹59/₹99/₹149/₹199)
3. Clicks a plan → QR code auto-generated from your UPI
4. User pays & clicks "PAYMENT DONE"
5. User sends screenshot
6. YOU get screenshot in Telegram with 3 buttons:
   ✅ APPROVE → sends private link to user
   ❌ REJECT → tells user payment rejected
   ⚠️ FAKE → warns user about fake payment
